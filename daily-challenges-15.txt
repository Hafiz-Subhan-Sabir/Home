# 15 daily challenges (5 categories × 3 moods)
# Format: Category → Mood → Challenge

Business → Happy → Write one concrete win from the last seven days and share it with one colleague or client so momentum becomes visible (specific, upbeat).
Business → Tired → In one 20-minute block, only clear quick wins: three unblock emails or three backlog tasks with dates; defer deep strategy and log what you postponed.
Business → Analytical → Map one noisy workflow (inputs, outputs, owners, lag) and name one metric to track next week with an owner for the readout.

Money → Happy → Consciously allocate a small, capped joy spend that fits your plan; write one line tying the spend to a value (not impulse).
Money → Tired → Five-minute safety: review subscriptions, pause one unused 60+ days if allowed, confirm next major bill date—no trading or new signups.
Money → Analytical → Build last month’s snapshot: inflows, fixed/variable outflows, savings rate; top three variable categories; one numeric lever for next month.

Fitness → Happy → Fifteen minutes of playful movement (dance, walk, light sport); log how mood shifted afterward.
Fitness → Tired → Eight minutes gentle mobility, full glass of water, set a lights-out time; note one thing you skip tonight to protect sleep.
Fitness → Analytical → Compare planned vs actual movement; adjust one numeric variable next week (frequency, duration, or rest) with a short rationale.

Power → Happy → Recall one boundary you held; distill it into a one-line principle; optional share with someone who reinforces your standard.
Power → Tired → Decline or reschedule one non-essential ask with one polite sentence; save the reply as a reusable snippet.
Power → Analytical → Stakeholder map for one monthly goal (decide/influence/block); draft three bullets for the highest-leverage conversation.

Grooming → Happy → Choose one outfit or grooming combo that signals who you’re becoming; wear it for a real interaction; optional photo.
Grooming → Tired → Minimal viable routine in ≤10 min: cleanse, moisturize, teeth, one neat detail—no perfectionism spiral.
Grooming → Analytical → Audit presentation vs one role you play; list three cheap, fast upgrades and schedule the easiest this week.
